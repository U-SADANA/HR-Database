SAMPLE HR PITCH

Call the HR [ office hours ]

Good Morning/Good Afternoon. Am I speaking to (Mr./Ms). (HR Name) from (COMPANY NAME)?

If yes

Hello, (Sir/Ma'am). My name is (YOUR NAME) and I am calling on the behalf of Mr. Muralidharan Sir, Chief Placement Officer, Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering, Sriperumbudur. Could I please borrow 5 minutes of your time?

If yes

Sir/Ma'am, we are organizing an event called the MOCK PLACEMENTS. This event is held exclusively for the pre-final year students of our college and assists them in preparing for the actual placements which will be held for them next year. The event aims to help students understand the requirements of the industry, by making them experience an one-on-one interview-like environment.

(Sir/Ma'am), this is the 20th successive year of our event and college would be extremely greatful to you and your company if you could take out some time on Sunday, the 23rd of February to interview our students and make them aware about industry requirements and areas of potential improvement.

The college shall be providing you a pick up and drop service from your desired location on the 23rd. The event starts from 9:00 A.M onwards and we will drop you back at your convenient time. We would also be arranging breakfast, lunch and mid-day snacks for you. You can choose to interview students from any department of your choice. The number of students to be interviewed is also totally upto you, (Sir/Ma'am). This event would also be an opportunity for you to gauge the level of the students and gain insights about our college.

Do you have any queries for me, (Sir/Ma'am)?

Refer FAQs

Could I please have your E-Mail ID so that I can send you a formal invite?

(Note down the mail ID)
Thank you so much for your time and patience, (Sir/Ma'am). We will shortly send you a mail regarding the event details. Also, wishing you a very happy new year, (Sir/Ma'am). (Pause for him/her to wish me back) Have a nice day, (Sir/Ma'am). Thank you.
(End Call)

If no

I am so sorry to disturb you, (Sir/Ma'am). What time would it be suitable to call you back?
(Note down time) Thank you, (Sir/Ma'am).
(End Call)
