FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What am I going to gain by coming to this event?

This event would also be an opportunity for you to gauge the level of the students and gain insights about our college. Also, you can offer internships and pre-placement offers to students whom you find competent. In addition to that, we will be hosting 100+ HR's, and thus, it will be a great opportunity for you to network and make contacts with fellow HR's.

Will I be provided with a single room to conduct interviews?

Is it alright if I get back to you on this later, (Sir/Ma'am)?

Will I paid for attending this event?

I am so sorry, (Sir/Ma'am). We usually do not pay HR's who help us make this event a grand success year after year. Nevertheless, if you have any other specific requirements, you can always reach out me at this number or my E-Mail.

If HR tries to put forth his own ideas like conducting a seminar or anything else

Sir, our students have already undergone an mock aptitude test and group discussion. We would be glad if you could come

How many students will I be allotted to interview?

There will be a certain number of students that we will allot to your company based on your department preferences and their aptitude scores. Approximately 15-20 students throughout the day.

If I'm in a management, can I still attend the event?

Of course sir/madam, we would be privileged to have you.

I will only interview a particular number of students

Of course sir/madam, we will arrange accordingly

Will I have to stay for the entire event? Will transport be arranged when I want to leave?

The event starts at 9:30 AM and ends at 3:30 PM. However you can leave any time you want and we will arrange the transport accordingly.

If the HR asks for unreasonable requests

Sir/madam we will get back to you on that.

If the unreasonable request cannot be fulfilled

No sir/madam, we will not be able to provide (whatever they ask). However we have (the alternate option we do provide)

Can I not represent my company?

Yes sir/madam, if you are comfortable representing your company. You are welcome to interview our students as a private consultant. We just want to fullfill our goal to give our students a learning experience.
